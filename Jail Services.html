Jail & Detention Center Notary Services

We understand the urgency and sensitivity involved in notarizing documents for individuals in custody. Signature Notary is experienced with navigating the procedures for secure facility visits in Santa Clara County, including San Jose Main Jail and Elmwood Correctional Complex.

What to Expect
  • ✔ Contact the jail first to confirm notary visits are allowed
  • ✔ Speak with the inmate before the visit so they understand what the visit is for and what to expect
  • ✔ Notaries cannot give legal advice or fill out forms — bring completed documents (except for signatures)
  • ✔ If witnesses are required, plan ahead — some jails only allow official personnel to enter
  • ✔ Provide jail-issued ID or wristband for the inmate; credible witnesses must show valid California ID
  • ✔ Some jails may only allow non-ink signatures — this is still legally binding
  • ✔ Decide ahead of time how you want the signed document returned (pickup, mail, or courier)
  • ✔ Arrive on time — delays may result in canceled appointments or additional fees
  • ✔ Notaries will cancel the appointment if the signer appears unaware, confused, or under duress
  • ✔ The notary cannot advise on which notarial act is needed — contact the receiving agency or an attorney for clarification

Book a Visit

To schedule a jail notary visit, please fill out the contact form on our Contact Page and include the following:

  • Full name and booking number of the inmate
  • Facility name (e.g., San Jose Main Jail, Elmwood)

  • Preferred appointment dates and times

We will respond promptly to confirm availability and coordinate with facility requirements.
