Our Services

At Signature Notary, we bring professionalism and peace of mind right to your doorstep. Below are the types of documents I commonly notarize — always with care, flexibility, and a smile.

Identity Verification Forms

Need to prove who you are? I can notarize identity-related forms for licensing, employment, or background checks.

Power of Attorney

Empower a loved one to act on your behalf with a notarized POA — explained clearly and handled with respect.

Personal Declarations & Forms

From travel consent forms to financial agreements — I help you get it signed, sealed, and ready to go.

Real Estate Documents

Need a grant deed or property-related form notarized? I travel to your location to get it done — quickly and clearly.

Acknowledgments & Jurats

Standard notarial acts done with clarity and professionalism — no stress, just smooth signing.

Mobile Appointments

Can’t travel? I come to you. Flexible scheduling and friendly service wherever you’re located.

Fee Schedule
  • Acknowledgments: $15 per signature
  • Jurats: $15 per individual taking oath or affirmation + certificate
  • Depositions: $30 for all services, $7 for oath, $7 for certificate
  • Certified Copy of Power of Attorney: $15 per copy
  • Journal Entry Copy: $0.30 per page

Maximum fees per California Government Code §8206 & §8211

Additional Fees
  • Mobile Travel Fee: Starting at $20 (based on distance)
  • Early Morning & Evening Appointments: +$15 for outside working hours
  • Rush Service: +$30 for urgent appointments

Additional fees are subject to change. I accept cash, Venmo, and Zelle.

Please note: I am not a loan signing agent. I do not prepare documents or give legal advice. All signers must present valid photo identification.
